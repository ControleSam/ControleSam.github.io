Intro

By formal training, I'm a Condensed Matter Physicist specialised in Computational Soft Matter. Over the years, I have developed a passion for a wide range of topics that involve computer programming and therefore have gathered experience in programming with different languages while still open to learning new technologies.

I currently work as an Optical Engineer at Dispelix, one of the leading Augmented Reality (AR) waveguide companies. Part of my work is the development of software for analysing our products, image processing, algorithm development, Critical to Quality documentation and perfoming simulations for the next generation AR glasses.

Outside of research and work, I am a lot of things, including an artist and I love to experiment with all kinds of traditional media. I recently exhibited some of my work in my community, Sprendlingen-Gensingen. I love digital art as well, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator and Blender3d being my go-to applications.

While you are still here, check out a few of my awesome work.

Professional Experience

I currently work as an Optical Engineer at Dispelix, one of the leading Augmented Reality (AR) waveguide companies.

My work includes:

  • Defining product specifications for the next generation AR glasses using Desing for Six Sigma tools
  • Using light propagation tools to design waveguides to suite our product specifications.
  • (Re)defining metrics, developing related algorithms and writing computer programs for develepment and analyses of our AR displays.
  • Image analyses and postprocessing
  • Documenting our CTQ

Prior to my current work, I have worked in an academic setting as an Early Stage Researcher. This position also paved the way for my doctoral studies.
